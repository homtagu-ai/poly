Refund & Cancellation Policy

Last updated: February 6, 2026

This Refund & Cancellation Policy outlines how subscription cancellations, refunds, and billing are handled for PolyHunter ("Service"). By subscribing, you agree to the terms described below.

Summary

  • Cancel anytime from your account settings or by emailing us
  • 7-day refund window on your first subscription payment
  • Access continues until end of billing period after cancellation
  • Renewal charges are non-refundable

1. Subscription Plans

PolyHunter offers the following subscription options:

PlanBilling CycleAuto-Renewal
MonthlyBilled every 30 daysYes, until cancelled
AnnualBilled every 12 monthsYes, until cancelled

All subscriptions automatically renew at the end of each billing cycle. You will be charged the current rate for your plan unless you cancel before the renewal date.

2. How to Cancel

You may cancel your subscription at any time using any of the following methods:

  • Account Settings: Navigate to Settings in your PolyHunter dashboard and click "Cancel Subscription"
  • Email: Send a cancellation request to contact@poly-hunter.com from the email address associated with your account

Cancellation requests are processed within 24 hours. You will receive an email confirming your cancellation.

3. What Happens After Cancellation

  • Your subscription remains active until the end of your current billing period
  • You retain full access to all paid features during this time
  • No further charges will be applied after cancellation takes effect
  • After the billing period ends, your account reverts to a free/limited state
  • Your account and data are preserved; you can resubscribe at any time

4. Refund Policy

4.1 First-Time Subscribers

If you are a first-time subscriber and are not satisfied with the Service, you may request a full refund within 7 days of your initial subscription payment. This applies to both Monthly and Annual plans.

4.2 Renewal Charges

Renewal charges (subsequent billing cycles) are non-refundable. It is your responsibility to cancel before the renewal date if you do not wish to continue.

4.3 Annual Plan Early Cancellation

If you cancel an Annual plan after the 7-day refund window, no partial or prorated refunds will be issued. You will continue to have access for the remainder of your annual billing period.

4.4 Exceptions

We may issue refunds outside the above policy at our sole discretion in the following circumstances:

  • Extended service outages (more than 72 consecutive hours) caused by us
  • Billing errors or duplicate charges
  • Where required by applicable consumer protection laws in your jurisdiction

5. How to Request a Refund

To request a refund, email contact@poly-hunter.com with:

  • Your account email address
  • Date of the charge
  • Reason for the refund request

Refund requests are reviewed and processed within 5-10 business days. Approved refunds are returned to the original payment method.

6. Free Trials

If a free trial is offered:

  • You will not be charged during the trial period
  • We will send a reminder before the trial ends
  • If you do not cancel before the trial expires, your subscription will automatically convert to a paid plan at the current listed price
  • The 7-day refund policy applies from the date of the first paid charge

7. Price Changes

If we change subscription pricing:

  • Existing subscribers will be notified at least 30 days in advance
  • New pricing takes effect at your next renewal date
  • You may cancel before the renewal to avoid being charged at the new rate

8. Chargebacks and Disputes

If you have a billing concern, we encourage you to contact us at contact@poly-hunter.com before initiating a chargeback with your bank or payment provider. We are committed to resolving billing issues promptly and fairly. Accounts associated with fraudulent chargebacks may be suspended.

9. Contact Us

For any billing, refund, or cancellation questions:

Email: contact@poly-hunter.com

Website: poly-hunter.com

We aim to respond to all billing inquiries within 24 hours.
