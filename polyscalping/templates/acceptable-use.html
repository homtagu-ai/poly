Acceptable Use Policy

Last updated: February 6, 2026

This Acceptable Use Policy ("AUP") governs your use of PolyHunter ("Service"). By accessing or using the Service, you agree to comply with this policy. Violations may result in suspension or termination of your account.

1. Permitted Use

The Service is designed for individual use by subscribers for the purpose of:

  • Analyzing prediction market events using AI-powered tools
  • Tracking market movements, whale activity, and orderbook data
  • Using position sizing calculators and risk assessment tools
  • Accessing educational analysis and AI-generated market insights

The Service is intended as an informational and educational tool. All trading decisions are made at your own risk and discretion.

2. Prohibited Activities

You agree not to use the Service to:

2.1 Illegal or Harmful Activities

  • Engage in any activity that violates applicable local, state, national, or international laws
  • Facilitate money laundering, terrorist financing, or other financial crimes
  • Manipulate prediction markets or engage in market manipulation schemes
  • Conduct fraud or misrepresent your identity

2.2 Unauthorized Access and Technical Abuse

  • Attempt to gain unauthorized access to the Service, other accounts, or our systems
  • Reverse-engineer, decompile, disassemble, or extract source code from the Service
  • Use automated scripts, bots, scrapers, or crawlers without explicit written permission
  • Circumvent rate limits, access controls, or any security measures
  • Introduce malware, viruses, or other harmful code
  • Overload, flood, or perform denial-of-service attacks on the Service

2.3 Commercial Misuse

  • Resell, redistribute, or sublicense access to the Service or any data obtained from it
  • Use the Service to provide competing analysis services to third parties
  • Share your account credentials with others or allow multi-user access on a single-user plan
  • Systematically download, copy, or export data from the Service for commercial use
  • Use the Service outputs to create derivative products or services without written permission

2.4 Content and Communication Abuse

  • Submit false, misleading, or fraudulent information to the Service
  • Harass, threaten, or abuse other users or our staff
  • Impersonate any person or entity
  • Post or distribute spam, phishing content, or malicious links

3. Account Responsibility

  • You are responsible for all activity under your account
  • You must keep your login credentials secure and not share them
  • You must use accurate information when creating your account
  • You must notify us immediately if you suspect unauthorized access to your account

4. Fair Usage

To ensure a quality experience for all users, the Service may implement reasonable usage limits including:

  • API request rate limits
  • Maximum number of analyses per time period
  • Data export limits

These limits are published in the Service documentation and may be adjusted from time to time. Attempting to circumvent these limits is a violation of this policy.

5. AI-Generated Content

When using AI-generated analysis and recommendations provided by the Service:

  • You understand that AI outputs are for informational purposes only and are not financial advice
  • You must not present AI-generated analysis as your own professional financial advice to third parties
  • You acknowledge that AI outputs may contain errors and should not be the sole basis for trading decisions
  • You are solely responsible for any actions taken based on the Service's outputs

6. Reporting Violations

If you become aware of any violation of this policy, please report it to contact@poly-hunter.com. All reports are reviewed and handled confidentially.

7. Enforcement

Violations of this Acceptable Use Policy may result in:

  • Warning: A notification to cease the prohibited activity
  • Temporary Suspension: Temporary restriction of access to the Service
  • Account Termination: Permanent removal of access without refund
  • Legal Action: Pursuit of legal remedies for damages caused by the violation

The severity of enforcement depends on the nature and extent of the violation. We reserve the right to take immediate action without prior warning in cases of severe violations.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Acceptable Use Policy from time to time. Material changes will be communicated via email or in-app notification. Your continued use of the Service after changes take effect constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
